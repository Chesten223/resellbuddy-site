FAQ

Is it safe for my Poshmark account?
ResellBuddy uses human-like random delays (3-8 seconds between actions) and stays within platform rate limits. We never ask for your password — it works with your existing logged-in session.
Do I need to keep my computer on?
Yes, like all Chrome extension tools, your browser needs to be open. Schedule your actions during the day while you use your computer for other things.
